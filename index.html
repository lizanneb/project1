My Favorite Celtic Star Dresses

Celtic Star is an Irish Dance costume design company created and run by Connor O'Sullivan; World Champion and teacher from the Scanlon School of Irish dance in the Midlands, England. Over the past few years, the designs that have been produced by the Celtic Star team have changed the aesthetic, style, and standard of the Irish dance dress and continue to leave dancers and judges speechless with theire sharp color combinations, innovative applique, bold embroidery, and of course tons of Swarovski crystals in assorted colors finish off every unique look.
